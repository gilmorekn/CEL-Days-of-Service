CELebrate Service!

Days of Service Program Overview

App State students are invited to get to know the High Country by stepping out into the Boone community and donating a few hours of time to a local non-profit.

Volunteers serve with a local Community Partner from 10am-1pm, then return to the CEL Outreach Center to gather for a time of reflection, lunch, and conversation.

To sign up for a Day of Service in Spring 2024, visit the CEL Engage page today!
